About me

Welcome! I am an assistant professor in the Department of Statistics at Rutgers University, New Brunswick. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics (Ph.D. minor in Statistics) from Stanford University, advised by Prof. Persi Diaconis. I obtained my bachelor's degree in School of Mathematical Science from USTC in June 2015. My research lies broadly in MCMC methods, Bayesian Statistics, statistical machine learning and applied probability.
