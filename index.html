About me

Welcome! I am an assistant professor in the Department of Statistics at Rutgers University, New Brunswick. My research lies broadly in MCMC methods, Bayesian Statistics, statistical machine learning and probability. I am also actively learning quantum computing recently. Feel free to contact me if you would like to chat about interests we may share.

My research is partially supported by NSF DMS-2210849. Thank you, NSF!
