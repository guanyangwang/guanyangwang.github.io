About me

Welcome! I am an assistant professor in the Department of Statistics at Rutgers University, New Brunswick. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Stanford University, advised by Prof. Persi Diaconis. My research lies broadly in MCMC methods, Bayesian Statistics, statistical machine learning and probability. I am also actively learning quantum computing recently. Feel free to contact me if you would like to chat about interests we may share.

My Erdős number is 2 .
